About Us

Founded in 2018 and nestled in the heart of Kurunegala, Sri Lanka, Glitzy Corner has been a trendsetter in the world of exquisite cake designs. Our passion for baking and creativity has driven us to craft over 50 unique cake designs that not only tantalize your taste buds but also serve as stunning centerpieces for your special occasions. At Glitzy Corner, we believe that every celebration deserves a touch of elegance and artistry. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision - to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through the art of cake making. From birthdays to weddings, anniversaries to corporate events, our cakes have been a part of joyous celebrations, adding that touch of sweetness that brings smiles and happiness to all.

What sets us apart is not just the delectable taste of our cakes, but the meticulous attention to detail in every creation. Each cake is a masterpiece, a labor of love, skillfully handcrafted by our team of expert bakers and decorators. We blend the finest ingredients with artistic flair, resulting in cakes that not only look like works of art but also taste heavenly.Over the years, Glitzy Corner has become synonymous with innovation and creativity. Our cake designs range from the traditional to the avant-garde, catering to a diverse array of tastes and preferences. We take pride in our ability to listen to our customers, understand their visions, and turn them into reality. Your dream cake is our mission, and we pour our heart and soul into every cake we create.

As we continue to grow, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering. We strive to push the boundaries of cake design, constantly experimenting with new techniques, flavors, and styles. Whether you're looking for a classic tiered cake or a whimsical custom creation, Glitzy Corner is here to make your cake dreams come true. Thank you for being a part of our journey. Join us in celebrating life's special moments with a touch of sweetness and a dash of artistry. Welcome to Glitzy Corner, where every slice tells a story and every bite is a memory to cherish.
